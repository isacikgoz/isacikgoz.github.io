Setting up Algo on AWS EC2 instance

İbrahim Serdar's Blog
2017-06-10

The need of VPN

Various. Seriously there are a lot of privacy issues around the world wide web and you want the keep your information with yourself. Also you don’t want to get raped by the ads. The latter is; I don’t want corporations to know me better than myself by tracking me.

First I was using OpenVPN as a VPN server. It was easy to configure and had a plenty of guides over internet. But, it was requiring a client software and I couldn’t manage to use it with my mobile phone.

And one day I was reading my twitter feed, my bro @fatih mentioned about Algo and I always trust his advices, therefore I gave a try to Algo.

Setting Up Algo

Initially I started with an Ubuntu 16.04 machine, it was a bit annoying at the beginning to be honest. I was following the instructions but there were plenty of errors on that journey. Luckily I managed to resolve those issues. I was working with a remote VPS and the pipe was not so stable therefore I was occasionally getting disconnected. macOS build was as easy as pulling a hair out of butter. Just followed the official instructions and viola!
